About Us

We are professional and experienced pet-sitter, pet trainer and dog-walker. Available for dog walks and drop-in visits, as well as overnight stays for traveling clients reluctant to leave their best friends unattended. We love and care deeply for all animals and believe in gentle handling and positive reinforcement as means of communicating, and training of household pets.

We have a ton of pet care experience from having pets of our own and being a profession for over 5 years. We are a team of clean, kind, responsible and understanding people and are eager to go above and beyond for our clients at all times; which includes some household duties such as cleaning after the animals, collecting mail, caring for plants. We are more than happy to do drop-ins, walking visits, light intermediate training sessions as well. We are capable of administering oral and topical medications, and of course providing companionship! We organize various events for your furry friend because we understand how much they mean to you!!
